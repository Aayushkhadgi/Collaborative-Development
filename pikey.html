Your Daily Schedule

* Day 01
Arrival in Tribhuvan International Airport, Kathmandu and transfer to Hotel
* Day 02
Preparation day in Kathmandu, Gear check and safety/trip briefing, Team meal
* Day 03
Drive from Kathmandu to Dhap via Jiri (2850m) by private vehicle or bus - Approx. 8-9 hours
* Day 04
Trek from Dhap to Japre (2820m) - Approx. 5-6 hours
* Day 05
Trek from Japre to Pikey Base Camp (3600m) - Approx. 5-6 hours
* Day 06
Trek from Pikey Base Camp to Pikey Peak (4065m) and descend to Loding (2740m) - Approx. 7-8 hours
* Day 07
Trek from Loding to Junbesi (2675m) - Approx. 6-7 hours
* Day 08
Trek from Junbesi to Thuptenchholing (2930m) - Approx. 5-6 hours
* Day 09
Trek from Thuptenchholing to Phaplu (2413m) - Approx. 5-6 hours
* Day 10
Drive from Phaplu to Kathmandu by private vehicle or bus - Approx. 9-10 hours
* Day 11
Leisure day in Kathmandu, sightseeing and exploring

The Manaslu Circuit Trek is a captivating journey around the eighth highest peak in the world, Mount Manaslu (8,163 meters), located in the remote Himalayan region of Nepal. This trek offers a pristine wilderness experience with fewer crowds compared to other popular treks like the Everest Base Camp or Annapurna Circuit.
